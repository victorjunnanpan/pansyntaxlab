Team members, Zetao Xu, Tingting Weng, Wanxin Li and Zhongyang Yu, are doing fieldwork for Qiangic languages in the West of Sichuan. The documentation and preservation of endangered languages are urgent! Our team works closely with Prof Yang Huang from Southwest Jiaotong University, who is an expert of Qiangic langauges.
