PhD student [Chenghao Hu](https://chenghao-hu.github.io//) has been visiting UCL for 6 months. He is under the CUHK Provost’s Strategic Allocation of Centrally-funded RPg Places – International Joint Supervision of PhD Students scheme, joint-supervised by Prof Victor Pan and Prof [Klaus Abels](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/7907-klaus-abels) from University College London, UK. During Prof Victor Pan's visiting at UCL, the three of them had a working session in Prof Klaus Abels' office.
