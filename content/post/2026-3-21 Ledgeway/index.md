Professor Adam Ledgeway (U. Bergamo) gives three lectures at the Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the Wilson T. S. Wang-New Method College Visiting Professorship in Language Education. 1. March 24: Romance Verb-movement and Parameters of Clausal Microvaration: Synchronic Variation and Language Contact. 2. March 25: Cultivating Linguistic Awareness in Language Education: Comparative Lessons from the Linguistic Tapestry of Chinese, Romance and Germanic. 3. March 30: At the Interfaces: When Syntax, Phonology, Pragmatics and Semantics Come Together.
